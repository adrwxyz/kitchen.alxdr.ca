# The Basic Salad

When you're in an ingredient pinch!

- Lettuce and/or greens
- Tomato
- Cucumber

# Elevated Salad

This is the real deal salad recipe!

- Lettuce
- Spinach
- Tomato
- Cucumber
- Onion
- Pomegranate
- Feta cheese (optional, add less salt in dressing)

# Dressing

A classic Alexander salad dressing!

- Olive oil
- Lemon
- Salt, to taste
