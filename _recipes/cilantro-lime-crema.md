total_time: '10'
servings: '8'
ingredients:
  - "1 bunch of chopped cilantro"
  - "Zest and juice of 2 limes"
  - "1 tbsp thick sour cream"
  - "2 tbsp mayo"
  - "2 cups yogurt (thinner, slightly tangy)"
  - "1 tsp sumac"
  - "1 tsp salt"
instructions:
  - "Chop the cilantro finely."
  - "In a medium bowl, combine the yogurt, sour cream, and mayo."
  - "Add the zest and juice of 2 limes to the mixture."
  - "Stir in the chopped cilantro, sumac, and salt."
  - "Mix well until all ingredients are fully combined."
  - "Taste and adjust seasoning as needed."
  - "Refrigerate for at least 15 minutes before serving to allow flavors to meld."
  - "Serve as a topping for tacos, burrito bowls, grilled meats, or vegetables."
---

A versatile and flavorful crema that adds a bright, tangy kick to any dish. The combination of lime, cilantro, and sumac creates a unique Middle Eastern twist on the classic Mexican crema.

Perfect for meal prep - this crema keeps well in the refrigerator for up to 5 days in an airtight container.
