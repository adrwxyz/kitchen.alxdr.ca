ingredients:
  - "1 head radicchio, quartered, cored, and roughly chopped"
  - "2 romaine hearts, chopped"
  - "3 pears, cored and thinly sliced"
  - "2 navel oranges, peeled and segmented"
  - "1 shallot, thinly sliced"
  - "1/2 cup walnuts, roughly chopped"
  - "100-150g Parmigiano-Reggiano, thick shaved"
  - "1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil"
  - "Juice of 1 lemon"
  - "2-3 tbsp fresh orange juice (reserved from segmenting oranges)"
  - "Zest of 1 orange"
  - "1 tbsp dark maple syrup"
  - "1/2 tbsp sumac"
  - "1 garlic clove, minced"
  - "Salt and black pepper, to taste"
instructions:
  - "Prepare the radicchio: Core and chop the radicchio, then soak it in ice water for 15-30 minutes to mellow the bitterness. Drain and pat dry."
  - "Chop the romaine hearts and set aside."
  - "Prepare the citrus: Peel and segment the oranges, reserving some juice for the dressing. Zest one orange before peeling."
  - "Make the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the minced garlic, lemon juice, reserved orange juice, orange zest, maple syrup, sumac, salt, and pepper. Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while whisking until emulsified."
  - "Assemble the salad: In a large bowl, combine the drained radicchio, chopped romaine hearts, sliced pears, orange segments, sliced shallot, and walnuts."
  - "Dress the salad: Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine."
  - "Finish with thick shaved Parmigiano-Reggiano on top and serve immediately."
---

Serves 6.

This vibrant winter salad combines the bitterness of radicchio with sweet pears and oranges, crunchy walnuts, and savory Parmigiano-Reggiano. The citrus maple dressing with sumac brings all the flavors together beautifully.
