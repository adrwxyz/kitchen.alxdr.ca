ingredients:
  - section: Sauce Base
    items:
      - "250g (½ tub) sour cream"
      - "1 tbsp mayo"
      - "1–2 lemons, juiced (to taste)"
      - "1 jalapeño, finely diced"
      - "1 shallot, finely diced"
      - "2–3 full stems cilantro, finely chopped"
  - section: Fajita Spice Blend
    items:
      - "3 tsp oregano"
      - "1 tsp paprika"
      - "½ tsp ground cumin"
      - "Pinch of cayenne"
      - "Salt and pepper, to taste"
instructions:
  - "Combine all spice blend ingredients in a small bowl and mix well."
  - "In a larger bowl, stir together the sour cream, mayo, lemon juice, jalapeño, shallot, and cilantro."
  - "Fold in the spice blend. Taste and adjust seasoning or lemon to preference."
  - "Use immediately as a sauce or refrigerate for up to 3 days. Also works as an overnight marinade for chicken."
