ingredients:
  - section: Sauce
    items:
      - "½ cup honey"
      - "¼ cup soy sauce"
      - "1 tsp fresh grated ginger"
      - "2 tbsp minced garlic (or ~6 cloves)"
      - "¼ tsp red pepper flakes (or 2 dried spicy red peppers)"
      - "½ tsp cornstarch, mixed with a splash of water before adding"
      - "Sprig of Thai basil"
  - section: Stir-fry
    items:
      - "1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined"
      - "Broccoli"
      - "Butter"
      - "Cooking oil"
      - "Salt"
      - "Pepper"
instructions:
  - "On high heat, sauté broccoli with salt and pepper in batches. Set aside."
  - "Add shallot with oil, cook briefly, then set aside."
  - "Add butter and shrimp to the pan. Add some sauce. If the shrimp releases a lot of liquid, remove shrimp and reduce or discard the watery sauce."
  - "Add remaining sauce to the pan with a little oil. Once bubbling, return everything — shrimp, broccoli, shallots. Toss and serve over rice."
