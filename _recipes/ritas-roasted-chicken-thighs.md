  - "2 tbsp olive oil"
  - "2 garlic cloves, crushed"
  - "Thyme, rosemary or oregano (or all)"
instructions:
  - "Wash chicken thighs with lemon and salt to reduce 'stinkiness'"
  - "Make your chicken marinade: lemon zest from 3 lemons, ⅓ cup of juiced lemon, 2 tbsp olive oil, 2 garlic cloves crushed, thyme, rosemary or oregano (or all)"
  - "Marinate for however long (recommended 2 hours)"
  - "Place in oven in dutch pan (no lid) at 425°F until golden, flipping halfway through"
  - "Finish with a sprinkle of salt"
