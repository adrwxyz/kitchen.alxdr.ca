servings: '4'
ingredients:
  - section: Chicken & Vegetables
    items:
      - 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts, sliced into strips
      - 3 bell peppers (mixed colours), sliced
      - 1 large onion, sliced
      - 2 tbsp olive oil
      - Flour or corn tortillas, for serving
  - section: Fajita Seasoning — Downshiftology
    items:
      - ½ tbsp chili powder
      - ½ tbsp ground cumin
      - 1 tsp garlic powder
      - ½ tsp paprika
      - ½ tsp dried oregano
      - ½ tsp kosher salt
      - ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  - section: Fajita Seasoning — Allrecipes
    items:
      - 1 tbsp cornstarch
      - 2 tsp chili powder
      - 1 tsp salt (or to taste)
      - 1 tsp ground paprika
      - 1 tsp white sugar
      - ½ tsp onion powder
      - ½ tsp garlic powder
      - ½ tsp ground cumin
      - ½ tsp cayenne pepper
instructions:
  - Combine all ingredients for your chosen seasoning blend in a small bowl and mix well.
  - Toss sliced chicken with the seasoning until evenly coated.
  - Heat olive oil in a large cast-iron skillet or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  - Cook chicken strips for 5–7 minutes, turning once, until cooked through and slightly charred. Remove and set aside.
  - In the same pan, sauté the peppers and onion for 5–6 minutes until softened and caramelised at the edges.
  - Return chicken to the pan, toss everything together, and cook for 1–2 minutes more.
  - Serve immediately in warm tortillas with desired toppings (sour cream, guacamole, salsa, shredded cheese, lime).
notes:
  - The Downshiftology blend is mild and clean — good for kids or spice-sensitive palates.
  - The Allrecipes blend uses cornstarch, which helps the seasoning cling to the meat and creates a slight sauce in the pan. Adds noticeable heat from cayenne.
