## Ingredients

### Chicken & Vegetables

- 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts, sliced into strips
- 3 bell peppers (mixed colours), sliced
- 1 large onion, sliced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Flour or corn tortillas, for serving

### Fajita Seasoning — Downshiftology

A clean, simple blend. Mild heat.

- ½ tbsp chili powder
- ½ tbsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp paprika
- ½ tsp dried oregano
- ½ tsp kosher salt
- ¼ tsp ground black pepper

### Fajita Seasoning — Allrecipes

Bolder blend with cornstarch, which helps the seasoning cling and creates a slight sauce. Adds a touch of heat from cayenne.

- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 2 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp salt (or to taste)
- 1 tsp ground paprika
- 1 tsp white sugar
- ½ tsp onion powder
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp ground cumin
- ½ tsp cayenne pepper

## Steps

1. Combine all seasoning ingredients in a small bowl and mix well.
2. Toss sliced chicken with seasoning until evenly coated.
3. Heat olive oil in a large cast-iron skillet or frying pan over medium-high heat.
4. Cook chicken strips for 5–7 minutes, turning once, until cooked through and slightly charred. Remove and set aside.
5. In the same pan, sauté peppers and onion for 5–6 minutes until softened and caramelised at the edges.
6. Return chicken to the pan, toss everything together, and cook for 1–2 minutes more.
7. Serve immediately in warm tortillas with desired toppings (sour cream, guacamole, salsa, shredded cheese, lime).
