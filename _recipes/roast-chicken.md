ingredients:
  - section: Chicken
    items:
      - "1 whole chicken, giblets removed and patted dry"
      - "1 lemon, halved"
      - "2 sprigs fresh thyme (optional)"
  - section: Butter Herb Mix
    items:
      - "½ cup butter, melted"
      - "Drizzle of olive oil"
      - "1 head of garlic, peeled and grated"
      - "2 sprigs fresh or crushed dry thyme"
      - "Zest of 1 lemon"
  - section: Potatoes
    items:
      - "6–8 potatoes, quartered"
      - "3 tbsp olive oil"
      - "Salt & Pepper"
instructions:
  - "Preheat oven to 500°F. Let the chicken sit at room temperature for about 30 minutes before roasting."
  - "In a large bowl, toss the potatoes with olive oil, salt, pepper, and thyme. Place in the bottom of a cast iron Dutch oven or lined metal tray."
  - "Combine butter mix ingredients. Rub generously over the chicken, stuffing some under the skin. Insert the lemon halves inside the cavity."
  - "Place chicken on top of the potatoes. Add thyme sprigs underneath if using. Tie legs together and tuck wings. Leave uncovered throughout cooking."
  - "Roast at 500°F for 20 minutes."
  - "Without opening the oven, reduce to 350°F and continue roasting 30–40 minutes, until a thermometer reads 165°F between thigh and breast."
  - "Remove from oven and rest at least 10 minutes before carving."
