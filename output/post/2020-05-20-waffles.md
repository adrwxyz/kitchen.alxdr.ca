## Ingredients

- 2 large eggs
- 2 cups flour
- 1 3/4 cup milk
- 1/2 cup oil
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 4 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp Vanilla extract
