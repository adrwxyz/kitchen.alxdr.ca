# Ingredients

- 1/4 cup olive oil (or neutral oil)
- 1/4 cup lemon juice
- 2 boiled potatoes, at room temperature
- 1 big clove of garlic, smashed
- 1/4 cup mayonnaise

# Steps

- Emulsify (by blending) oil and lemon juice
- Add rest of ingredients and blend
