# Ingredients

* Spinach
* Pomegranate, garnish

# Dressing

* Cashew
* Water
* Pomegranate molasses
* Salt (only add if using unsalted cashews)

# Steps

* Blend dressing ingredients 
* Toss spinach in dressing
* Top generously with pomegranates
